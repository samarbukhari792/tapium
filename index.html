Solo Development Vision

Tapium is crafted entirely by Sayyed Bukhari, an independent developer who believes in creating authentic gaming experiences. Every feature is designed with player satisfaction and fair monetization in mind.

The app features a comprehensive achievement system, tag unlocks, and competitive elements inspired by popular games like PUBG, 8-Ball Pool, and Snapchat Avatars.

Community-driven development ensures that player feedback directly shapes the future of the game, making it a truly collaborative experience.
