About Tapium

Tapium Protocol is revolutionizing the world of cryptocurrency mining by making it accessible to everyone through an engaging tap-to-earn system. We believe that the future of mining should be democratic, fun, and rewarding for all participants.

Built with cutting-edge technology and a vision for mass adoption, Tapium transforms the complex world of crypto mining into an intuitive mobile experience. Built by The 4th Oath - a team dedicated to decentralization and financial freedom.

Core Features

Coin per Tap System

Earn coins with every tap. Simple, intuitive, and rewarding gameplay mechanics designed for maximum engagement.

VIP Boosters

Unlock exclusive VIP tiers with enhanced mining rates, special bonuses, and premium features for dedicated users.

Referral Rewards

Build your network and earn passive income through our comprehensive referral program with multi-tier rewards.

Manual KYC & Real-Time Payments

Secure identity verification and instant payment processing ensure trust and reliability for all users.
